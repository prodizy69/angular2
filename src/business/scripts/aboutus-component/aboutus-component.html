About BayBoon

U.S. based BayBoon is an e-commerce engine and destination to purchase a variety of products and services anytime at discounted prices. BayBoon is an open platform to grab products or services at the best price in the form of "deals" or get even better prices if purchasing in a "group." BayBoon's platform also functions as a mobile app showcasing deals to customers in a single click. BayBoon is specific to localities featuring local service offerings for each location. Customers can enjoy holiday or weekend getaways at affordable prices by picking the destination from BayBoon's travel catalog. Our product and service listings are curated so our customers get the best possible deal quotes. BayBoon offers varied products and services ranging from electronics to fashion home needs to healthcare local services to travel and more.

BayBoon emerged to re-structure the business framework and consolidate merchants in an open platform. BayBoon strives to serve both customers and sellers equally, for example the "group buying" feature is just one example of BayBoon's commitment to benefit sellers by engaging a bigger customer base while offering customers the savings provided by group discounts. BayBoon also offers enhanced features related to direct mail campaigns and marketing strategies to help sellers' businesses grow and operate more efficiently.
