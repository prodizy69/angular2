Sign Up

How to sign up with BayBoon for business account?

  • Login to and click on create business account.
  • Login to BayBoon consumer link and click on Business signup
    • Note:- Business details submitted cannot altered again. In order to make changes submit a request with BayBoon admin through

Sign In

How to sign in with BayBoon for business account?

  • Go to and click on business signIn to login.
  • Enter your business account email ID and password.

Deals

How to post a product deal ?

Click on deals -> Create new deal
  • Go to preview to look at how deal is posted, BayBoon spoc will also review the deal and curate to grab customer attention.
  • Post updation preview, click on submit for review

How to post a service deal ?

Click on deals -> Create new deal

Deal Draft

  • Any deal either in product or service could be saved as draft till you publish

Pending Deal

  • Deal submitted for review will be verified by BayBoon admin

Approved Deal

  • All the approved deals will be listed under approved deals
    • Important note :- Any changes to deals which are already posted live, will follow the same approval process.

Posted Deal(Live)

  • A deal approved by BayBoon admin will be published live based on start time - end time given in the deal.
    • Important note :- Any changes to deals which are already posted live , will follow the same approval process.

Consumer Related

Verify consumer info

  • Can verify the redeem coupon when the customer visits to avail the service.
  • If you have multiple service deals posted, select the appropriate deal and enter deal ID to verify.

Purchased Product Deal

  • Each product deal purchased by consumers will be listed in here with the details of shipping address to which the product has to be shipped Important note :- Once a product is shipped to consumer, status has to be updated here with a tracking ID of shipping service to get to know live transit status time to time.

Returns

  • All the product Return requests will be updated in the Returns tab.
  • Returns tab is a framework to track / update / status check and finalize on payments.
  • Pending Returns will have the requests awaiting action to be taken by the Seller/Bayboon Admin.
  • Approved Returns are return requests processed successfully.

Reports

Reports

Three different reporting frameworks are designed to simplify the transaction updates with Bayboon and consumer.

  • Shipping & Tracking - After the consumers procures a product deal , product shipment and tracking is visible under this section.
  • Payments - Payment updates related to procured product & service deals will be reflecting under Payments section.
  • Returns - Only the return requests will be reflecting under returns section.

My Account

Business Info

  • Change Password - To change the password if necessary
  • Add Bank Account - Add the bank account details to which payments have to deposited.
